Summary - Data Visualization and Storytelling
The Power BI project explores a Superstore sales dataset to uncover key insights about sales trends, profitability, and product performance across regions and categories. The report includes five visualizations

1. Monthly Sales Trend
2. Profit by Region
3. Sales vs Profit by Category
4. Top 10 Products by Sales
5. Profit vs Discount Scatter Plot

Interactive filters allow users to explore the data by Region, Segment, or Category. The final dashboard provides a comprehensive overview for business decision-making.
